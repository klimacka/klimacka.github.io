About

I am a web developer with full-stack background that has been lately focusing mainly on frontend development. I prioritize comfortable UX, maintainability, efficiency and code reuse.

I've been doing web stuff for about 20 years, 15 years commercially, so not only do I know the new stuff buzzing right now, but I've been doing the job when IE 6 and PHP 4 were around and Chrome was not yet a thing.
